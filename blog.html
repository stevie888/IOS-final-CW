The word Bonsai actually refers to the japanese art of cultivating and growing beautiful small trees which replicates the shape of full sized trees in small container. Bonsai specimens are ordinary trees and shrubs(not hereditary dwarf)that are dwarfed by a technique of pruning roots and branches.The positive and negative impact of keeping bonsai at home ;

Positive impact

Bonsai plants are a beautiful way to bring the positive energy and nature to the home.One of the most adored and sought after benefits of bonsai trees is that they help in upplifting the beauty of your home in a very subtle way.Different types of bonsai trees provided discrete appeal to home decor.Bonsai has multiple health benefits that promotes a healthy lifestyle. Bonsai plants helps to purifying air,produce oxygen which is beneficial for human health.It is also believed that bonsai can help in lowering blood pressure and reduces the psychological stress. There are several types of bonsai trees, each trees has its own symbolic meaning.Depending on a person's culture of beliefs, bonsai trees are symbol of harmony,balance,patience or even luck.There are many benefits of bonsai plants that impact our daily life.Nowadays bonsai trees are enjoyed and love by many people all over the world.

Negative Impact

As i mentioned above the positive impact of bonsai,Bonsai also have negative impact in our lives.The major disadvantages of bonsai plants are that they require constant care and attention,need watering daily,can be expensive and have been linked with increase insect infestation which can infect several diseases.Bonsai requires alot of work such as watering which needs to be done daily or even twice a daily in some instances,which is definitely concerning if you donot have a lot of free time.
